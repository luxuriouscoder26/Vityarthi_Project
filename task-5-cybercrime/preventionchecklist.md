# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments  
- Verify emails, messages, and websites before sharing personal information  
- Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches  
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone  

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required to send money  
- Always verify payment requests before approving them on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept collect requests from strangers  
- Avoid sharing screen or installing unknown apps during calls related to payments  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance  

## ⚠️ Final Reminder
- Stay alert, think before you click, and always verify before you trust online sources.
